Google Chrome overtakes Internet Explorer as the Web’s most used browser.

I remember when Chrome first came out, I gave it a try but it was full of bugs so I quickly went back to Opera. I gave it another try few years ago and it has been my favorite ever since.

I’m surprised Opera is so low in this graph though, it really is a great browser but according to the stats it is less popular than Safari? 

As a developer it makes my heart happy to see people switching from IE to Chrome/Firefox. Hope this trend continues.
